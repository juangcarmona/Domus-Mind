# Domain Overview

## Purpose

DomusMind models the **operational reality of a household**.

The domain represents the people, time, resources, and logistics that together form the daily functioning of a family system.

---

## The Household as a System

A household is a living system composed of interacting elements:

- people
- time
- tasks
- routines
- resources
- obligations
- assets
- logistics
- relationships

DomusMind maintains a structured representation of this system so it can be understood, coordinated, and anticipated.

---

## Core Domain Model

### Family

A **Family** represents the primary operational unit.

A family contains:

- members
- dependents
- pets
- shared resources

---

### Members

A **Member** is a person belonging to a family.

Members participate in:

- events
- tasks
- routines
- schedules

---

### Areas

An **Area** is a lightweight classification used to make household activity easier to scan.

Examples include:

- house
- pets
- family
- leisure
- admin

Areas do not model ownership or accountability.
They simply help organize plans, tasks, and timeline entries.

---

### Time and Planning

Household life is structured around time.

DomusMind models time through:

- events
- schedules
- tasks
- routines
- reminders

These elements combine into the **family timeline**, which represents the operational flow of the household.

---

### Tasks and Routines

Tasks represent actions that must be completed.
Routines represent predictable recurring behavior within the household.
